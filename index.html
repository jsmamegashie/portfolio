Who I Am

Strategist by training.
Builder by instinct.

I am Joanita Senam Akushika Amegashie, digital marketing strategist, educator, entrepreneur, and nonprofit founder. Based in Accra. Working everywhere. You know me as Madam Digital.

I started in the classroom, studying education and information at the University of Ghana, and never lost the instinct to teach. Today that shows up everywhere: in the strategy decks I write, the workshops I run, and the brands I help build.

I run Ravvis Media with my co-founder, lead campaigns under Madam Digital, formulate clean beauty at Numa Organics, and pour into young people through GIVE and Mova Africa.

Currently completing my MA in Digital and Strategic Marketing Management at UPSA, because the playbook keeps changing and I want to keep being useful.

Certified by Harvard / Aspire Institute, Meta, Yale, Northeastern University and UC Davis.
